Pocket Cinema (PCNH)

Pocket Cinema is a learning-first creative tool built to help people use AI with clarity, intent, and consistency — not guesswork.

Created by Nathan Histed • © 2025 • All rights reserved

What it does

  • Turns simple ideas into strong, structured prompts
  • Uses task-based tabs (Logo, Enhance, Album, Mockup, etc.)
  • Teaches by guiding you: focus points, avoid list, and examples
  • Works across platforms (Gemini, LM Arena, Sora, CapCut, ChatGPT, etc.)

Why it exists

  • Most people only use a small fraction of what AI can do
  • Too many tools add options instead of understanding
  • Pocket Cinema focuses on constraints — because constraints create consistency

Privacy & safety

  • Client-side: your images stay in your browser
  • No accounts, no logins
  • If you choose AI rewrite, you bring your own API key
